Behind what I do

Hey, I'm Geraldine

I’m a graphic designer with three years of experience in commercial interior design. Having worked across both spatial and visual design, I see myself as a multidisciplinary designer who approaches projects with structure, curiosity, and intention. My background in interiors has trained me to consider space, user experience, function, materials, and the small details that shape how people feel.

As I move deeper into graphic and brand design, I’m drawn to creating visual identities, campaigns, and experiences that feel thoughtful, meaningful, and human. I believe good design lives in the details: the way elements are arranged, how a message is framed, and how people connect with it. Whether through branding, illustration, or experience-led design, I aim to create work that communicates clearly and leaves a lasting impression.
