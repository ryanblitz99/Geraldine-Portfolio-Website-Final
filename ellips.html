Ellips
Packaging

Reimagining Ellips hair-vitamin packaging as a clearer, more convenient and Gen Z-friendly product system created for the TikTok Shop experience.

The Challenge

Ellips was recognisable for its colourful hair-vitamin capsules, but the existing format could feel inconvenient, messy and difficult to understand at a glance. The challenge was to make the product easier to use while communicating its purpose clearly within the fast-paced TikTok Shop environment.

The Approach

We translated the iconic oval capsule form into a pump dispenser bottle that supports cleaner and more controlled application. A clear “Who, What and Why” information system was introduced to communicate the brand, product function and key ingredients immediately.

Project Highlights

Clearer Product Communication

A clearer hierarchy and structured information system make the product easy to understand at a glance.

Portable & User-Friendly

Pump dispenser and sample-pack solutions are neat, convenient and easy to carry and use.

Retail & Campaign Ready

A flexible system designed to work across product packaging, display visuals and TikTok Shop promotion.

Outcome & Reflection

The final system offers a more hygienic and straightforward hair-care experience while retaining Ellips’ recognisable oval identity and pink brand language. The pump bottle reduces mess and improves dosage control, while the sample pack allows users to test the product and carry it while travelling.
