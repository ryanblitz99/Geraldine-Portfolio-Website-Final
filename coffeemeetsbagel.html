Coffee
Meets Bagel

Reimagining the dating-app experience through a gamified reward system that encourages meaningful interaction, continued engagement and shared storytelling.

The Challenge

Dating apps often become repetitive and transactional, causing users to lose interest before meaningful connections can form. The challenge was to encourage continued participation without making dating feel overly competitive or superficial.

The Approach

We introduced a gamified reward feature that transforms everyday interactions into a progressive journey. The experience uses incentives, milestones and storytelling to motivate users while retaining Coffee Meets Bagel’s focus on thoughtful connections.

03 — Development

Process & Exploration

The project developed through research, interface exploration, gamification strategy and campaign touchpoints. Select a chapter to view the process in greater detail.

Outcome & Reflection

The final concept extends Coffee Meets Bagel beyond a conventional matching platform by making participation feel more rewarding and engaging. The project explored how gamification can support an emotional experience without distracting from the goal of forming genuine connections.
